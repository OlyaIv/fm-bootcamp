A small peak into my life outside working hours

Beyond my professional pursuits, I'm open for trying out new things in life, finding inspiration in myself and what I'm into currently.

For a very long time I was ashamed of not being super pro at one thing, and the fact that I didn't have one hobby to be consestent in. But I was lucky enough to realise that trying yourself in different things in your life is also valuable.

I have tried myself in makeup artistry, pole dancing, baking, high heels dancing, singing and little bit of painting. I'm not a pro but I'm having fun!
